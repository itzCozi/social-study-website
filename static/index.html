All That Existed 600-1000 CE

Tang Song Dynasty
The Sui Dynasty unified China but later succumbed to rebellion, leading to the establishment of the Tang Dynasty by Li Yuan, who claimed descent from Laozi. Emperor Taizong, his son, expanded Buddhism while Wu Zetian, his concubine, rose to power, briefly establishing the Zhou Dynasty before the Tang resumed control, making her the sole female emperor in Chinese history.
